# Calgary LEGO Train Club (CLTC)

This is a draft GitHub Pages site created for discussion purposes.

## About / Mission

The Calgary LEGO Train Club (CLTC) is a community of LEGO train builders and operators in the Calgary area. We build layouts, share building techniques, and run trains at public exhibitions to inspire fans of all ages.

Our goals are:
- Provide a welcoming entry point for new LEGO train fans
- Collaborate on large layouts and displays
- Show safe operation and good display etiquette
- Celebrate member creativity and club history

## How to Join

New members are welcome—no formal train experience required.
- Send a message via our club social channel (or email).
- Volunteer for 2 shows a year.
- Review the Code of Conduct and display rules.
- Bring something you’re working on (optional—building is encouraged but not required).

### Time & commitment: 
Members are asked to contribute at least one of:
- bringing a train/module to a display
- helping with set‑up/tear‑down
- monitor and interact with crowds.

## Leadership & structure
CLTC operates with a small leadership team to keep communications, events, and member experience consistent.

### Roles:
- Community Ambassador: LEGO program contact and policy compliance
- Events Lead: coordinates show layouts, times, set‑up and tear‑down
- Web/Social Lead: posts monthly updates and maintains archives

## Code of Conduct
CLTC is a family-friendly community. By participating in meetings or displays, you agree to:
- Be respectful and inclusive
- No harassment or bullying
- Keep language and topics appropriate for all ages
- Follow safety rules at displays and respect venue policies
- Share credit and ask permission before copying someone’s designs
- Escalation: warnings → removal from event/club participation
